## Collaboration Workflow and Communication Plan

### Daily Communication
- **Daily Standups**: 15-minute meetings at 10:00 AM
- **Communication Channels**: 
  - Primary: WhatsApp group for quick updates
  - Secondary: Email for formal communications
  - Development: GitHub for code-related discussions

### Weekly Coordination
- **Weekly Planning**: Every Monday at 2:00 PM
- **Progress Review**: Every Friday at 4:00 PM
- **Demo Sessions**: Every Friday after progress review

### Decision-Making Process
1. **Technical Decisions**: Team Leader with input from relevant specialists
2. **Design Decisions**: UI/UX Designer with team feedback
3. **Architecture Decisions**: Team Leader and Backend Developer
4. **Priority Decisions**: Team Leader with team consensus

## Escalation and Support Procedures

### Issue Escalation Path
1. **Level 1**: Peer support within functional area
2. **Level 2**: Team Leader involvement
3. **Level 3**: Team discussion and collaborative resolution
4. **Level 4**: External consultation if needed

### Support Mechanisms
- **Buddy System**: Each team member has a designated backup
- **Knowledge Base**: Shared documentation of solutions and procedures
- **Regular Check-ins**: Proactive identification of support needs
- **Flexible Role Support**: Team members can assist outside primary roles when needed

## Performance Monitoring and Evaluation

### Individual Performance Metrics
- **Task Completion Rate**: Percentage of assigned tasks completed on time
- **Code Quality**: Code review scores and bug rates
- **Collaboration Score**: Peer feedback on teamwork and communication
- **Learning and Growth**: Skill development and knowledge sharing contributions

### Team Performance Metrics
- **Sprint Velocity**: Story points completed per sprint
- **Quality Metrics**: Bug rates, code coverage, performance benchmarks
- **Communication Effectiveness**: Meeting efficiency, decision-making speed
- **Deliverable Quality**: Stakeholder satisfaction with deliverables

### Regular Review Process
- **Weekly Individual Check-ins**: Progress, challenges, and support needs
- **Bi-weekly Team Retrospectives**: Process improvement and team dynamics
- **Monthly Performance Reviews**: Individual and team performance assessment
- **Project Milestone Reviews**: Comprehensive evaluation at major milestones
